Project Concept

I will design and develop a responsive website for a client who builds custom decks. The primary objectives of the website are:
    • To attract new clients by showcasing the builder’s craftsmanship and capabilities.
    • To present a portfolio of past projects, allowing prospective customers to evaluate the quality and style of the work.
    • The website will cater to individuals seeking affordable, high-quality custom decks. It will be responsive, ensuring optimal viewing and usability across all devices.

Site Map

The site will include on-site navigation with hyperlinks to the following main sections:

  1. Home: index.html
    • Homepage – Introduction and overview of services.
  2. About us: About.us.html
    • About Us – Background information on the builder and business values.
  3. Images/ gallery: images.html
    • Gallery/Images – A visual portfolio of completed deck projects.
  4. Styles and layouts: Layouts.html
    • Styles & Layouts – Descriptions and images of available deck styles and design options.
  5. Prices: prices.html
    • Prices – General pricing information or estimates.
  6. Checkout: checkout.html
    • Checkout – For project deposits or service bookings, if applicable.
  7. Contact us: contact.us.html
    • Contact Us – A form or contact details for inquiries and consultations.
